About Me

Please realize as a reader I can identify issues that are going on in your life but I am not a therapist. Readings do not always bring favorable news and I cannot compromise the truth to protect someone’s feelings.

After you have had a reading please understand that you and you alone are responsible for the choices you make. If you do things that are contrary to the reading it does change the outcome of the situation. It could be an action or a conversation without you realizing it until it’s too late, so be aware of your conversations and actions.

Please know that if something or someone is out of your reach and the window of opportunity is missed I will not mislead you or encourage you to move forward. I provide help so you become less dependent on me and rely on yourself and your decisions.

We are responsible for our own actions and reactions and no one else’s. And of course, free will plays a big part in all of this.
